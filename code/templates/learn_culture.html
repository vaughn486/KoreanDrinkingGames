A night out is done in rounds (each round is a "cha"). Korea has a culture of drinking - don't eat too much at the 1st
round if you know there's more "cha's" to come
Example of a night out:

1-cha ("il-cha")
1-cha (1st round) at a Korean
bbq restaurant with soju
2-cha ("i-cha")
2-cha (2nd round) at a
Pojangmacha aka Pocha restaurant with SoMaek and Kimchi Pancakes
3-cha ("sam-cha")
3-cha (3rd round) at Karaoke
with more drinks and tteok-bokki
